THRED VISION

THRED VISION is a readymade fashion garments manufacturer. We supply readymade garments to Brand Studio LifeStyle Private Limited of the domestic sector and Lindsey Apparels of Exports.

THRED VISION started the journey in the garment industry by Mr. Subhash N (the founder) in May 2023 with a clear international mission of making world-class garments and to be perceived as the best in the garment industry and for the expansion into the vision of best quality garments all over our limits.

Mr. Subhash N and Mr. Sandesh N as board of Directors. We value the environment and meet the needs of our esteemed customers by delivering the exceptional quality of formal, semi-formal, Casual shirts and Kurthis, at an unparalleled price and lead time.

Our production capacity currently stands at 3.6 lakh units of garments per annum and 2.5, which we aim to expand to larger numbers. Thred Vision currently has about 200 employees constituting tailors, sewing facilitators and staff to run operations and maintain quality of products.
